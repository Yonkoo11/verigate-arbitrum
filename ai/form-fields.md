## Project Introduction

Verigate is a compliance layer for tokenized real-world assets on BNB Chain. There's $3B in RWA on BNB Chain and no native compliance standard. Existing projects use simple address whitelisting — works for mint/redeem, breaks the moment tokens hit DeFi. You can't trade a whitelisted TBILL on PancakeSwap without compliance checks on the buyer. We built modular compliance contracts that check BAS (BNB Attestation Service) attestations on every token transfer. Three modules ship today: country restriction, accredited investor verification, and holder cap enforcement. Issuers deploy a compliant token with all modules in one transaction via our factory contract. We build on BAS — BNB Chain's native attestation service — not Chainlink CCID or Tokeny's ONCHAINID. One KYC attestation lets an investor access every compliant RWA token on BSC. Deployed on BSC testnet, verified on BSCScan, core compliance flow working end-to-end.
